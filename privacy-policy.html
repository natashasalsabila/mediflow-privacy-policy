Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how MediFlow handles account and queue information.

About MediFlow

MediFlow is a clinic queue management application designed to help patients book queues and help clinic staff manage queue services more efficiently.

Information We Collect

MediFlow may collect user account and queue-related information, such as:

  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Gender, birth date, and address
  • Selected clinic or polyclinic
  • Queue number, booking date, and booking time
  • Queue status and notification-related queue information

How We Use Information

The collected information is used only for:

  • Account management, login, and authentication
  • Queue booking and queue status display
  • Clinic service and admin queue management
  • Patient notifications and improving the clinic queue experience

Data Storage and Authentication

MediFlow uses Supabase for authentication and data storage. User data is stored and managed through Supabase services.

Data Sharing

MediFlow does not sell user personal data to third parties. Clinic staff or administrators may access patient and queue information only for clinic service and queue management purposes.
